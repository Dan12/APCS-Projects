Minesweeper

Mines are randomly hidden underneath the squares- uncover all the non-mine squares to win the game.


Left-click on a covered square:

  • Uncover a mine and you lose the game.
  • Uncover a number that tells you how many mines are hidden in the immediately adjacent covered squares.
  • Uncover a blank square and all the immediately adjacent squares will automatically be uncovered (and if any of those squares are blank the process continues).


Right-click on a covered square:

  • The first right-click places a flag (the square cannot be uncovered while flagged).
  • The second right-click places a question mark (the square can be uncovered in this state).
  • The third right-click clears the square (the square can be uncovered in this state).


Double left-click on a numbered square:

  • If you have flagged fewer than this number of immediately adjacent squares, nothing happens.
  • If you have flagged at least this number of immediately adjacent squares, then the non-flagged immediately adjacent covered squares will be cleared.
